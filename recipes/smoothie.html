Smoothie

Description

This is how you make the best smoothie ever. You first add a ton of frozen berries. Then you add some vanilla protein powder. Then you add a spoonful of peanut butter, and you keep the peanut butter coverd spoon on standby, so that each bite has the taste of it. Then you add milk, blend, and devour.

Ingredients

  • Frozen berries
  • Milk
  • Protein Powder
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Berries in
  2. Protein powder in
  3. Milk and peanut butter in
  4. Blend
  5. Enjoy!
